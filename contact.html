Frequently Asked Questions

What is a fractional CFO?

+
A fractional CFO provides part-time, strategic financial leadership to businesses that need CFO-level expertise without the full-time salary commitment. You get the same calibre of financial strategy as a Fortune 500 company, scaled to your business.

How much does it cost?

+
Engagements start from $3,000/month for retainer arrangements, or from $5,000 for project-based work. Interim CFO placements are priced based on scope and duration. Every engagement is customised — we'll discuss pricing in your consultation.

How quickly can you start?

+
For most engagements, we can begin within one to two weeks of agreement. For urgent situations — pending transactions, compliance deadlines, or interim coverage — we can mobilise within days.

Do you work with businesses outside Sydney?

+
Absolutely. While we're based in Sydney, we work with businesses across Australia and internationally. Most of our work is conducted remotely with periodic in-person sessions as needed.

What industries do you specialise in?

+
Our experience spans manufacturing, tourism, professional services, technology, and e-commerce. The financial principles we apply are universal, but we bring particular depth in businesses with seasonal revenue patterns, M&A activity, or complex systems requirements.
