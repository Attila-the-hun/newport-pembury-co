Our Story

Every growing business reaches a point where the financial decisions become too complex for the founding team alone, but too early for a full-time CFO. Newport Pembury & Co exists for that moment.

We provide the strategic financial leadership that transforms uncertainty into clarity, complexity into confidence, and ambition into actionable growth. Whether you're navigating an acquisition, restructuring debt ahead of a sale, managing seasonal fluctuations, or implementing systems to scale, we sit in the CFO seat beside you.

The difference we bring is hands-on experience across the entire financial spectrum—from day-to-day cash flow management to enterprise-scale transactions. We've been in the room when it matters.

Our Heritage

Newport Pembury & Co carries a name with meaning and history. It honours Rev. Mapson Williams, Tim's great-grandfather—a Welsh rugby international who played for Newport RFC and earned his Wales cap in the 1923 Five Nations Championship, a signal achievement in an era when rugby defined national pride.

After his playing years, Mapson answered a different calling, becoming an Anglican minister serving communities in southern Sydney. His wife, Ruth, played the treadle organ at St. Stephens, weaving faith and service into the fabric of local life. Their legacy was one of discipline, excellence, and quiet service—values that demanded integrity in all things and a commitment to serving something larger than oneself.

The Newport Pembury & Co name carries forward that legacy of discipline, service, and quiet excellence. We bring the same standards to your financial leadership—rigorous, principled, and utterly reliable.
